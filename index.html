About

Syndéo facilitates the storage of your data in such a snormalystematic and simplified manner, on which you can run further analysis and take intelligent data-driven decisions for the improvement in quality and resources of your institute. With best in class cybersecurity on the platform, with GDPR compliance keeping the International Standards, this product has been born out of inputs from stakeholders of some of the most esteemed institutions in the country, ensuring the perfect solution for the management of official data and ensuring that the ownership and accessibility of the data stay with Institue and its staff alone.

Built on much easy to use the platform, and taking into considering that our users get the best experience in using our product, we have gone a step ahead and ensured that one can simply signup and start storing their data, with easy share options which produce logs of activity done on the go.

Connected with one of the most prestigious audit systems, “NAAC”, Syndéo provides seamless data entry into SSR, AQAR, and other documents which facilitate the process of audit. Syndéo ensures that all your data is stored at one location and links with various smart tags, enabling super fast search and accessibility, while reduces double data entry.

The Team of Syndéo is working relentlessly with some of the best people in the country, to give you many more audit systems, solutions tailor-made for an instruction that can be easily used for internal/external audits.
